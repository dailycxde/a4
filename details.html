What is Iron Man?

Iron Man is a popular Marvel Comics character created by Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, Don Heck, and Jack Kirby. He first appeared in Tales of Suspense #39 in 1963. The character, Tony Stark, is a wealthy industrialist and genius inventor who creates a high-tech suit of armor to become the superhero Iron Man. Stark uses his technology and intellect to fight crime and protect the world. Iron Man is also a key member of the Avengers in the Marvel Universe.

Important Links
